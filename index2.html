About Me

I'm Felipe José Bravo Márquez, I was born in 3th January 1984 on Santiago, Chile. I studied simultaneously Computer Science Engineering and Industrial Engineering both at the Universidad de Chile.

I've recently completed a Master in Computer Science at the same university under the supervision of Marcelo Mendoza and Barbara Poblete.

I also work as research assistant at Yahoo! Labs Latin America.

My areas of interest are:

  • Data Mining/Machine Learning

  • Information Retrieval

  • Sentiment Analysis

  • Social Media
