Affiliations

  • Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Chile.
  • Young Researcher at Millennium Institute for Foundational Research on Data (Funded by Programa Milenio, Chile).
  • Honorary Research Associate in the Machine Learning Group at the University of Waikato in New Zealand (Aotearoa). This is the lab were I did my PhD under the supervision of Bernhard Pfahringer and Eibe Frank.
  • Member of the REpresentation LEarning and Natural LAnguage Processing (ReLeLa) research group at DCC Uchile.

Research Areas

My research interests and expertise lie in the acquisition of knowledge and information from unstructured data, particularly natural language text, spanning the following overlapping fields:

  • Natural language processing
  • Data mining
  • Data science
  • Machine learning
  • Information retrieval
  • Social media analytics
  • Computational social science

My main research goals are to study society by computationally analysing traces left by humans in the digital space.
